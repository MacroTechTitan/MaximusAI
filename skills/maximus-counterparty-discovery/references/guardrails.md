# Guardrails

- Use public, licensed, consented, or user-connected data only.
- Do not defeat access controls, scrape prohibited pages, impersonate users, purchase leaked data, or collect credentials.
- Do not create dossiers based on protected characteristics, health, religion, politics, sexuality, or other sensitive personal traits.
- Do not export exact home addresses, family-member identities, personal phone numbers, private emails, or location routines merely because they appear in records.
- Do not target people because they suffered a bankruptcy, fraud, lawsuit, bereavement, or other vulnerability. Litigation records may establish relationships or institutional history, but outreach must be independently appropriate.
- Respect opt-outs, suppression lists, platform terms, privacy law, anti-spam law, securities/commodities rules, and firm policies.
- Label all wealth, accreditation, liquidity, intent, and suitability inferences. Require human verification before outreach or onboarding.
- For CTA/CPO work, verify registrations and disciplinary history through official regulator sources and route promotional use through compliance review.
